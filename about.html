ABOUT KAJI CORPORATIONS

Kaji Corporation is a leading provider of top-tier gaming setups, consoles, and peripherals. Our mission is to deliver the ultimate gaming experience to enthusiasts around the world. From high-performance gaming PCs to cutting-edge consoles and accessories, we offer a wide range of products to meet the needs of every gamer.


Kaji Corporations' cutting-edge hardware includes top-tier gaming peripherals and high-performance gaming laptops.

Kaji Corporations' software platform, with over 200 million users, features Kaji Connect (an advanced networking platform), Kaji Chroma RGB (a proprietary RGB lighting technology system compatible with thousands of devices and numerous games/apps), and Kaji Stream (a powerful game streaming service).

Kaji Corporations also offers comprehensive payment solutions for gamers, youth, millennials, and Generation Z. Kaji Credits is one of the leading game payment services worldwide, while Kaji Paytech provides paytech solutions in emerging markets.

Founded in 2019, Kaji Corporations is headquartered in Manila (Philippines), with regional offices in Berlin, Singapore and Tokyo. With a presence in several key locations worldwide, Kaji Corporations is rapidly emerging as a prominent brand for gamers, establishing itself in North America, Asia-Pacific,
and Europe.
